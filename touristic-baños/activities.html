What to do?

Baños de Agua Santa offers you a lot of entertainment, for kids, teens or adults, whether you like extreme sports or long walks or just enjoy a view or if you like to take photos. Here are three activites, with a lot of variety, know them and enjoy the pictures.

Enjoy this atraction with all your family, this forest that you can visit a day or night (recommend you at night) has a path that is ligthen by lamps, and leads you to many places, inside it you can found places like "the lost desert", "The house of Drako the Dragon", "The burrow of goblins". This attraction can be found at the center of Baños, right up in hill, it doesn't cost much, $4 for adult and $2 kids.

If you a like a little or feel some kind of adrenaline, this attraction is perfect for you, The swing of the end of the world, is a 30 meter string swing, is almost at the top of the moutain, and has a spectacular view of Baños, if you need a good photo for you social media here is your chane, professional photographers help you to have a photo in this places and it only costs $1, I warn you, the line of waiting can be long.

A peaceful place, for family to enjoy a view, a sacred place to good for your shoes (literally) you need to take your shoes off, before entering, is part of the policy of this place, the line is not too long, and you can take as many picture as you want, it is awesome by night but at day you can see better the view. In the same place that this attraction is founded, you can found another extreme activity that is "the fly of the condor", more info about it in my Personal Experience page.
